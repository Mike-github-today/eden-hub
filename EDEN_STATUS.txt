PREVIOUS SESSION: Clinical Governance ("Silent Auditor") [COMPLETED]

## Session Summary

Implementation of clinical governance features to support Eden's vicarious
responsibility under BACP, UKCP, and HCPC guidelines. This enables tracking of
supervision, DBS, and ICO registration compliance for associates.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FEATURES IMPLEMENTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Supervision Tracker
   - Added supervision fields to associates (supervisor name, accreditation,
     frequency, last confirmed date)
   - Status calculation: Current, Due Soon, Overdue
   - Supervision Card in Associate Profile > Compliance Section
   - "Confirm Supervision" button updates last confirmed date
   - Friction Feed integration for Due Soon/Overdue items

2. DBS Update Service
   - Added DBS fields (certificate number, certificate date, Update Service ID,
     enrolled status, last checked date)
   - Support for both Update Service (annual) and legacy 3-year cycle
   - Status calculation: Valid, Renewal Soon, Expired/Check Due
   - DBS Card in Associate Profile > Compliance Section
   - "Mark as Checked" button (Admin only)
   - Friction Feed integration for renewal reminders

3. ICO Registration
   - Added ICO fields (registration number, verified status, expiry date)
   - Status calculation: Valid, Renewal Due, Expired, Not Registered, Pending
   - ICO Card in Associate Profile > Compliance Section
   - Friction Feed integration for non-compliant states
   - Stage 2 onboarding gate: ICO registration now required
